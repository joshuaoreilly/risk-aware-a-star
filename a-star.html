A-Star

This is a variant of A-Star (A*) that has an additional penalty term for entering a "high risk" area. It also has a maximum range a path can be, but this combination breaks A-Star, and doesn't work properly.

Dark blue is a "keep out" area that cannot be entered, light blue is a "high risk" area that can be entered, but incurs a penalty and should be avoided.
